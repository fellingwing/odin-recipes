Garlic Kale Recipe

Garlic kale is garlic, olive oil, and minced garlic cooked together.

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Minced Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Clean the kale by soaking it in a bowl of water.
  2. Remove stems from kale by chopping them off.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet.
  4. Stir in garlic and cook until sizzling.
  5. Add kale to the skillet, toss, cover, and cook (tossing occasionally with tongs) until the kale is tender and vibrant green.
